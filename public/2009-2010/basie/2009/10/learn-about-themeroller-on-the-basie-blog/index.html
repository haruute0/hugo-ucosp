13 Oct 2009, 23:08

On the Basie blog I recently made a post about the jQuery UI Themeroller tool and why it’s useful. One of my purposes for making the post was to draw some input about the current color theme in Basie. As developers we don’t have to make these types of decisions every day, but it’s still quite an important one. At the very least take a look and learn a bit about jQuery UI, you might find it useful some day down the road!
