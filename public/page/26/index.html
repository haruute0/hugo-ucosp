Grading

A couple of students have asked how UCOSP projects are graded. The short answer is, you tell us. Seriously—talk to your mentors and say, “These are the things I want to tackle, here’s why I think they’d be valuable and how much effort they’ll require, so please give me grades for X, Y, and Z.” If your mentor thinks you’ve chosen things that are too hard, too easy, or not particularly useful, the two of you can negotiate back and forth until you settle on something that makes you both happy.
