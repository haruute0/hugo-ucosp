GSoC 2010

Our project, Mercurial, already announced their participation in Google’s Summer of Code 2010 via the developer mailing list. I suspect many other projects are doing the same, so even though there’s no official information on Google’s own SoC page (yet), you might want to consider preparing your application. If you’ve enjoyed UCOSP so far and want to continue the experience, GSoC looks like a great avenue to do so.
