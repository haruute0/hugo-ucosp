Why Isn&#039;t My School Listed?

A couple of people have emailed to ask why their university isn’t included in the list of who’s taking part in Winter 2010. The answer is that we haven’t been able to find a faculty member willing to anchor things there, or that no students have stepped forward (usually because they’re already in year-long capstone projects). All hope is not yet lost, though: we’d be happy to hear from anyone who’d like to join us, even at this late date.
