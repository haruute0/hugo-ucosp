Basie progress

Basie team is working hard to make 0.6 happen; Greg Wilson will be presenting this release at the PyCon 2010 in Atlanta. We seem to inhabit our mailing list, with occasional posts to the Basie blog, where we collect our status reports. Besides fixing many general bugs, we are concentrating on the usability, compatibility, and appearance improvements.
