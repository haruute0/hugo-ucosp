Coast to Coast&#8230; to Coast

We’re very pleased to welcome our first students from Carleton University in Ottawa, and Memorial University in Newfoundland—and not just because this means we’ll have students this term who can dabble their toes in three different oceans :-).  Ah, if only there was a university that bordered the Arctic Ocean…

This brings the total number of students to 46, which is nearly double our original target of two dozen. The budget for bringing them together Sept 25-27 won’t stretch much further, so we’re going to close the door on enrolment for the fall in just a few days.  Students will start posting their biographies soon after that.
